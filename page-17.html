What questions to ask before marriage from spouse ?

  1. How much financial contribution will be made by each of them?
  2. How daily food will be made, who will take responsibility to do it?
  3. How many children they want to support?
  4. What they will do in case one of them dies?
  5. How they will take care of parents?
