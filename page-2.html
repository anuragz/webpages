False Hope

Many times we keep false hope or we just want something to happen without doing any thing about it. This short story tells what a false hope is and how it ruins the little good that was possible.

There was a man he was very ambitious. Once while returning from work he found some seeds. He has never seem similar seeds before. Anyway he planted them with in short time the sprouts started to come out of soil. He was very happy he asked one of his friends and he told him that it looks like a special kind of fruit tree. The man become very happy he started to water the plant every day. The sprouts become plant and plant become tree. The man was eagerly waiting for the tree to bear the special fruits. He used to sit in trees shades in afternoon and started to take extra care of tree thinking what if in his absence someone else takes the fruits. With passing years his desire to eat the special fruits was growing more and more. After waiting for many years when the tree bear no fruits. Man started to sit under the tree shade and complain "I water you and protect you but you never give me any fruits, when will you bear the sweet fruits". Man stopped watering the tree and one day he become so furious in anger that he cut the tree.

The man was keeping false hope because it was not an edible fruit bearing tree, it was a Champak tree that he cut and lost the shade also in desire to get sweet fruits.
