Lessons learned from "The Richest Man in Babylon"

The simple and powerful book about personal finance, anybody can read and understand it and practise the ideas mentioned in this book.

  1. Save 10% of your income and do not spend it
  2. Invest the savings in profitable business, find a person(example - potter who want to buy mud can take loan from you) and give him some of the money and ask him to return back the money with interest
  3. Do not spend the investment profits, but put them into new investments
  4. Do not investment all your money in same business or person
