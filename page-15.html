Important lesson to learn from Osho

  1. There is no need for any competition with anybody. You are yourself, and as you are, you are perfectly good. Accept yourself.
  2. Life in itself has no meaning. Life is an opportunity to create meaning.
  3. Try to understand the word ‘faith’ does not mean ‘belief’. Belief is dogma … Faith is personal
