How to make life easy?

Some days back I read Buddhas teaching and learned a good thing that this world is filled with dukha( problems) but we can solve them.

  1. Do exercise or walk and get healthy
  2. Sleep properly, means sleep in night from 11 to 6 AM at least.
  3. Eat and drink juice, water, coconut water, anything good you can get in fare quantity
  4. Take care of your body needs
  5. Read good books and talk about it may be with wife, father, brother or any friend
  6. Walk or run or do something when you start feeling lonely
